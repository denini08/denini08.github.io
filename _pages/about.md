Hi there! I’m serving as an Assistant Professor at the [University of Pernambuco (UPE)](https://www.upe.br).

I obtained my Ph.D. in Computer Science from the [Federal University of Pernambuco (CIn-UFPE)](https://www.cin.ufpe.br) in March 2026, under the supervision of [Prof. Dr. Marcelo d’Amorim](https://damorim.github.io/). My doctoral research focused on software testing, especially challenges related to flaky tests and test reliability. I have been actively involved in academic research since 2020.

My research interests include:

- Software Engineering
- Programming Languages
- Software Testing
- Software Development
